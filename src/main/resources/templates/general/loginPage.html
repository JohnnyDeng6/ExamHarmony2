Exam Scheduling in Your Hands

Our approach to exam scheduling places you in control, allowing for fast, seamless, and flexible scheduling:

  • As an administrator, ExamHarmony gives you the tools to construct exam schedules according to your constraints.
  • As an instructor, ExamHarmony gives you ability your preferred request exam slots
  • As an invigilator, ExamHarmony allows you to seamlessly declare times which you are available for proctoring
